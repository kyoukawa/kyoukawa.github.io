# 琴生不等式

a,b,c∈R+,a21+a2+b21+b2+c21+c2=1,求证abc≤24a,b,c\in R^+,\frac{a^2}{1+a^2}+\frac{b^2}{1+b^2}+\frac{c^2}{1+c^2}=1,求证abc\le \frac{\sqrt{2}}{4} a,b,c∈R+,1+a2a2​+1+b2b2​+1+c2c2​=1,求证abc≤42​​

法1:令x=a2,y=b2,z=c2∴原命题  ⟺  xyz≤18∴x1+x+y1+y+z1+z=1∴11+x+11+y+11+z=2经展开,2xyz+xy+yz+zx=1≥42(xyz)34∴xyz≤18法1: \\ 令x = a^2,y = b^2,z = c^2 \\ \therefore 原命题\iff xyz \le \frac{1}{8} \\ \therefore \frac{x}{1+x}+\frac{y}{1+y}+\frac{z}{1+z} = 1 \\ \therefore \frac{1}{1+x}+\frac{1}{1+y}+\frac{1}{1+z} = 2 \\ 经展开,2xyz+xy+yz+zx = 1 \ge 4\sqrt[4]{2(xyz)^3} \\ \therefore xyz \le \frac{1}{8} \\ 法1:令x=a2,y=b2,z=c2∴原命题⟺xyz≤81​∴1+xx​+1+yy​+1+zz​=1∴1+x1​+1+y1​+1+z1​=2经展开,2xyz+xy+yz+zx=1≥442(xyz)3​∴xyz≤81​

法2:令x=a21+a2,y=b21+b2,z=c21+c2∴x+y+z=1,a2=x1−x,b2=y1−y,c2=z1−z原命题  ⟺  a2b2c2≤18  ⟺  x1−x⋅y1−y⋅z1−z≤18x1−x⋅y1−y⋅z1−z=xy+z⋅yx+z⋅zx+y≤x2yz⋅y2xz⋅z2xy=18(均值不等式)法2: \\ 令x = \frac{a^2}{1+a^2},y = \frac{b^2}{1+b^2},z = \frac{c^2}{1+c^2} \\ \therefore x+y+z = 1,a^2=\frac{x}{1-x},b^2=\frac{y}{1-y},c^2=\frac{z}{1-z} \\ 原命题 \iff a^2b^2c^2 \le \frac{1}{8} \iff \frac{x}{1-x}\cdot\frac{y}{1-y}\cdot\frac{z}{1-z} \le \frac{1}{8} \\ \frac{x}{1-x}\cdot\frac{y}{1-y}\cdot\frac{z}{1-z} = \frac{x}{y+z}\cdot\frac{y}{x+z}\cdot\frac{z}{x+y} \le \frac{x}{2\sqrt{yz}}\cdot\frac{y}{2\sqrt{xz}}\cdot\frac{z}{2\sqrt{xy}} = \frac{1}{8} (均值不等式) \\ 法2:令x=1+a2a2​,y=1+b2b2​,z=1+c2c2​∴x+y+z=1,a2=1−xx​,b2=1−yy​,c2=1−zz​原命题⟺a2b2c2≤81​⟺1−xx​⋅1−yy​⋅1−zz​≤81​1−xx​⋅1−yy​⋅1−zz​=y+zx​⋅x+zy​⋅x+yz​≤2yz​x​⋅2xz​y​⋅2xy​z​=81​(均值不等式)

法3:令a=tan⁡α,b=tan⁡β,c=tan⁡γ∴tan⁡2α1+tan⁡2α+tan⁡2β1+tan⁡2β+tan⁡2γ1+tan⁡2γ=1∴sin⁡2α+sin⁡2β+sin⁡2γ=1∴cos⁡2α=sin⁡2β+sin⁡2γ≥2sin⁡βsin⁡γ(均值不等式)同理可证,cos⁡2β≥2sin⁡αsin⁡γ,cos⁡2γ≥2sin⁡αsin⁡β∴cos⁡2αcos⁡2βcos⁡2γ≥2sin⁡βsin⁡γ⋅2sin⁡αsin⁡γ⋅2sin⁡αsin⁡β=8sin⁡2αsin⁡2βsin⁡2γ∴tan⁡2αtan⁡2βtan⁡2γ≥18法3: 令a = \tan\alpha,b = \tan\beta,c= \tan\gamma \\ \therefore \frac{\tan^2\alpha}{1+\tan^2\alpha}+\frac{\tan^2\beta}{1+\tan^2\beta}+\frac{\tan^2\gamma}{1+\tan^2\gamma}=1 \\ \therefore \sin^2\alpha+\sin^2\beta+\sin^2\gamma = 1 \\ \therefore \cos^2\alpha = \sin^2\beta+\sin^2\gamma \ge 2\sin\beta\sin\gamma(均值不等式) \\ 同理可证,\cos^2\beta\ge2\sin\alpha\sin\gamma,\cos^2\gamma\ge2\sin\alpha\sin\beta \\ \therefore \cos^2\alpha\cos^2\beta\cos^2\gamma \ge 2\sin\beta\sin\gamma\cdot2\sin\alpha\sin\gamma\cdot2\sin\alpha\sin\beta = 8\sin^2\alpha\sin^2\beta\sin^2\gamma \\ \therefore \tan^2\alpha\tan^2\beta\tan^2\gamma\ge\frac{1}{8} \\ 法3:令a=tanα,b=tanβ,c=tanγ∴1+tan2αtan2α​+1+tan2βtan2β​+1+tan2γtan2γ​=1∴sin2α+sin2β+sin2γ=1∴cos2α=sin2β+sin2γ≥2sinβsinγ(均值不等式)同理可证,cos2β≥2sinαsinγ,cos2γ≥2sinαsinβ∴cos2αcos2βcos2γ≥2sinβsinγ⋅2sinαsinγ⋅2sinαsinβ=8sin2αsin2βsin2γ∴tan2αtan2βtan2γ≥81​

赫尔德不等式Hölder

ai,bi(1≤i≤n)∈R+,α>0,β>0,α+β=1则∑i=1naiαbiβ≤(∑i=1nai)α⋅(∑i=1nbi)βa_i,b_i(1\le i\le n)\in R^+,\alpha >0,\beta >0,\alpha + \beta = 1 \\ 则\sum_{i=1}^n a_i^\alpha b_i ^\beta \le (\sum_{i=1}^n a_i)^\alpha\cdot (\sum_{i=1}^n b_i)^\beta \\ ai​,bi​(1≤i≤n)∈R+,α>0,β>0,α+β=1则i=1∑n​aiα​biβ​≤(i=1∑n​ai​)α⋅(i=1∑n​bi​)β

∑i=1naiαbiβ≤(∑i=1nai)α⋅(∑i=1nbi)β  ⟺  ∑i=1naiαbiβ(∑i=1nai)α⋅(∑i=1nbi)β≤1  ⟺  ∑i=1n(ai∑j=1naj)α(bi∑j=1nbj)β≤1ln⁡((ai∑j=1naj)α(bi∑j=1nbj)β)=αln⁡ai∑j=1naj+βln⁡bi∑j=1nbj≤ln⁡(αai∑j=1naj+βbi∑j=1nbj)由ln⁡x单调性,(ai∑j=1naj)α(bi∑j=1nbj)β≤αai∑j=1naj+βbi∑j=1nbj(琴生不等式)∴∑i=1n(ai∑j=1naj)α(bi∑j=1nbj)β≤∑i=1nαai∑j=1naj+βbi∑j=1nbj=α+β=1\sum_{i=1}^n a_i^\alpha b_i ^\beta \le (\sum_{i=1}^n a_i)^\alpha\cdot (\sum_{i=1}^n b_i)^\beta \\ \iff \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n a_i^\alpha b_i ^\beta}{(\sum_{i=1}^n a_i)^\alpha\cdot (\sum_{i=1}^n b_i)^\beta} \le 1 \\ \iff \sum_{i=1}^n (\frac{a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j})^\alpha (\frac{b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j})^\beta \le 1 \\ \ln ((\frac{a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j})^\alpha (\frac{b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j})^\beta) = \alpha \ln\frac{a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j}+\beta\ln \frac{b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j} \le \ln(\frac{\alpha a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j}+\frac{\beta b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j}) \\ 由\ln x单调性,(\frac{a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j})^\alpha (\frac{b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j})^\beta\le\frac{\alpha a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j}+\frac{\beta b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j} (琴生不等式)\\ \therefore \sum_{i=1}^n (\frac{a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j})^\alpha (\frac{b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j})^\beta\le\sum_{i=1}^n \frac{\alpha a_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n a_j}+\frac{\beta b_i}{\sum_{j=1}^n b_j} = \alpha + \beta = 1 \\ i=1∑n​aiα​biβ​≤(i=1∑n​ai​)α⋅(i=1∑n​bi​)β⟺(∑i=1n​ai​)α⋅(∑i=1n​bi​)β∑i=1n​aiα​biβ​​≤1⟺i=1∑n​(∑j=1n​aj​ai​​)α(∑j=1n​bj​bi​​)β≤1ln((∑j=1n​aj​ai​​)α(∑j=1n​bj​bi​​)β)=αln∑j=1n​aj​ai​​+βln∑j=1n​bj​bi​​≤ln(∑j=1n​aj​αai​​+∑j=1n​bj​βbi​​)由lnx单调性,(∑j=1n​aj​ai​​)α(∑j=1n​bj​bi​​)β≤∑j=1n​aj​αai​​+∑j=1n​bj​βbi​​(琴生不等式)∴i=1∑n​(∑j=1n​aj​ai​​)α(∑j=1n​bj​bi​​)β≤i=1∑n​∑j=1n​aj​αai​​+∑j=1n​bj​βbi​​=α+β=1
