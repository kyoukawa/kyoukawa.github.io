三角函数

Posted on 2023-01-25 Edited on 2023-09-12 In 旧博客搬运

诱导公式

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\sin (\pi + \alpha) = -\sin \alpha \
\cos (\pi + \alpha) = -\cos \alpha \
\tan (\pi + \alpha) = \tan \alpha
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\sin (\frac{\pi}{2}+\alpha) = \cos \alpha \
\cos (\frac{\pi}{2}+\alpha) = -\sin \alpha \
\tan (\frac{\pi}{2}+\alpha) = -\cot \alpha
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\sin (-\alpha) = -\sin \alpha \
\cos (-\alpha) = \cos \alpha \
\tan (-\alpha) = -\tan \alpha
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\sin (\pi - \alpha) = \sin \alpha \
\cos (\pi - \alpha) = -\cos \alpha \
\tan (\pi - \alpha) = -\tan \alpha
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\sin (\frac{\pi}{2}-\alpha) = \cos \alpha \
\cos (\frac{\pi}{2}-\alpha) = \sin \alpha \
\tan (\frac{\pi}{2}-\alpha) = \cot \alpha
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

和差角公式

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\sin (\alpha + \beta) = \sin \alpha \cos \beta + \cos \alpha \sin \beta \
\sin (\alpha - \beta) = \sin \alpha \cos \beta - \cos \alpha \sin \beta
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\cos (\alpha + \beta) = \cos \alpha \cos \beta - \sin \alpha \sin \beta \
\cos (\alpha - \beta) = \cos \alpha \cos \beta + \sin \alpha \sin \beta
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

$$
\left{
\begin{aligned}
\tan (\alpha + \beta) = \frac{\tan\alpha +\tan\beta}{1-\tan\alpha\tan\beta}\
\tan (\alpha - \beta) = \frac{\tan\alpha -\tan\beta}{1+\tan\alpha\tan\beta}
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

二倍角公式

$$
\sin 2\alpha = 2\sin \alpha \cos \alpha$$$$
\cos 2\alpha = 2\cos ^2\alpha -1$$$$
\cos 2\alpha = 1-2\sin ^2\alpha$$$$
\cos 2\alpha = \cos ^2\alpha -\sin ^2\alpha $$$$
\tan 2\alpha = \frac{2\tan\alpha}{1-\tan ^2\alpha}
$$

半角公式

$$
\sin \frac{\alpha}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos\alpha}{2}} \
\cos \frac{\alpha}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1+\cos\alpha}{2}} \
\tan \frac{\alpha}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos\alpha}{1+\cos\alpha}} \
\therefore \tan \frac{\alpha}{2} = \frac{\sin\alpha}{1+\cos\alpha} = \frac{1-\cos\alpha}{\sin\alpha}
$$

万能公式

$$
\sin 2\alpha = \frac{2\tan\alpha}{1+\tan ^2\alpha} \
\cos 2\alpha = \frac{1-\tan ^2\alpha}{1+\tan ^2\alpha}
$$

辅助角公式

$$
a\sin x+b\sin y = \sqrt{a^2+b^2}(\frac{a}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}\sin x+\frac{b}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}\cos x)
$$
$$
令\cos\theta = \frac{a}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}, \sin\theta = \frac{b}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}
\theta :辅助角
$$
$$
\therefore 原式=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}(\cos\theta\sin x+\sin\theta\cos x) \
=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\sin (x+\theta)\in [-\sqrt{a^2+b^2},\sqrt{a^2+b^2}]
$$

三倍角公式

$$\sin 3\alpha = 3\sin\alpha - 4\sin ^3 \alpha$$
$$\cos 3\alpha = -3\cos\alpha + 4\cos ^3\alpha$$
