$$y’= \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{d}{dx}f(x) = f’(x)$$

$$\therefore dy = f’(x)dx$$

积分

不定积分

求原函数
$$
dF(x) = f(x)dx \
\int f(x)dx = F(x)+C
$$

$$
\int \frac{dx}{x} = ln|x|+C
$$

换元积分

$$
求\int x\sin x^2 dx \
令u=x^2,du=2xdx \
\therefore \int x\sin x^2 dx = \int \frac{1}{2}\sin u du
=\frac{1}{2}\int\sin u du = -\frac{1}{2}\cos x^2 + C \
$$

举个例子:
$$
\int \frac{\cos\ln x}{x}dx\
=\int\cos\ln xd\ln x \
=\sin\ln x+C
$$

定积分

$$
\int f(x)dx = F(x) + C\
\int _a^b f(x)dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$
