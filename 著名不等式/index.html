琴生不等式

1

\[ a,b,c\in R^+,\frac{a^2}{1+a^2}+\frac{b^2}{1+b^2}+\frac{c^2}{1+c^2}=1,求证abc\le \frac{\sqrt{2}}{4} \]

\[ 法1: \\ 令x = a^2,y = b^2,z = c^2 \\ \therefore 原命题\iff xyz \le \frac{1}{8} \\ \therefore \frac{x}{1+x}+\frac{y}{1+y}+\frac{z}{1+z} = 1 \\ \therefore \frac{1}{1+x}+\frac{1}{1+y}+\frac{1}{1+z} = 2 \\ 经展开,2xyz+xy+yz+zx = 1 \ge 4\sqrt[4]{2(xyz)^3} \\ \therefore xyz \le \frac{1}{8} \\ \]

\[ 法2: \\ 令x = \frac{a^2}{1+a^2},y = \frac{b^2}{1+b^2},z = \frac{c^2}{1+c^2} \\ \therefore x+y+z = 1,a^2=\frac{x}{1-x},b^2=\frac{y}{1-y},c^2=\frac{z}{1-z} \\ 原命题 \iff a^2b^2c^2 \le \frac{1}{8} \iff \frac{x}{1-x}\cdot\frac{y}{1-y}\cdot\frac{z}{1-z} \le \frac{1}{8} \\ \frac{x}{1-x}\cdot\frac{y}{1-y}\cdot\frac{z}{1-z} = \frac{x}{y+z}\cdot\frac{y}{x+z}\cdot\frac{z}{x+y} \le \frac{x}{2\sqrt{yz}}\cdot\frac{y}{2\sqrt{xz}}\cdot\frac{z}{2\sqrt{xy}} = \frac{1}{8} (均值不等式) \\ \]

\[ 法3: 令a = \tan\alpha,b = \tan\beta,c= \tan\gamma \\ \therefore \frac{\tan^2\alpha}{1+\tan^2\alpha}+\frac{\tan^2\beta}{1+\tan^2\beta}+\frac{\tan^2\gamma}{1+\tan^2\gamma}=1 \\ \therefore \sin^2\alpha+\sin^2\beta+\sin^2\gamma = 1 \\ \therefore \cos^2\alpha = \sin^2\beta+\sin^2\gamma \ge 2\sin\beta\sin\gamma(均值不等式) \\ 同理可证,\cos^2\beta\ge2\sin\alpha\sin\gamma,\cos^2\gamma\ge2\sin\alpha\sin\beta \\ \therefore \cos^2\alpha\cos^2\beta\cos^2\gamma \ge 2\sin\beta\sin\gamma\cdot2\sin\alpha\sin\gamma\cdot2\sin\alpha\sin\beta = 8\sin^2\alpha\sin^2\beta\sin^2\gamma \\ \therefore \tan^2\alpha\tan^2\beta\tan^2\gamma\ge\frac{1}{8} \\ \]

2

\[ a_1,a_2,\cdots,a_n>0,求证\sqrt[n]{a_1a_2\cdots a_n}\le\frac{a_1+a_2+\cdots+a_n}{n}\\ 设f(x) = \ln x \\ \therefore f''(x) = -x^{-2} < 0 \\ \therefore f(x)上凸 \\ \because f(x)单调 原不等式 \iff \frac{1}{n}(f(a_1)+f(a_2)+\cdots+f(a_n)) \le f(\frac{a_1+a_2+\cdots+a_n}{n}),即琴生不等式 \]

3

\[ a,b,c>0,求证(abc)^{\frac{a+b+c}{3}}\le a^ab^bc^c \\ \]

\[ 法1: 设f(x) = x\ln x \\ \therefore f'(x) = 1 + \ln x ,f''(x) = \frac{1}{x} > 0 \\ \therefore f(x)下凸 \\ 由\ln x单调,原命题\iff \frac{a+b+c}{3}\ln(abc)\le f(a)+f(b)+f(c) \\ 由三元均值,abc\le(\frac{a+b+c}{3})^3 \\ \therefore \frac{a+b+c}{3}\ln(abc) \le 3f(\frac{a+b+c}{3}) \\ 由琴生不等式, 3f(\frac{a+b+c}{3})\le f(a)+f(b)+f(c) \\ \]

\[ 法2: \because a,b,c轮换对称 \\ \therefore 不妨设a\le b\le c \\ \because a,b,c > 0 \\ 原命题\iff a^{\frac{b+c-2a}{3}}\cdot b^{\frac{a+c-2b}{3}}\cdot c^{\frac{a+b-2c}{3}} \le 1 \\ a^{\frac{b+c-2a}{3}}\cdot b^{\frac{a+c-2b}{3}}\cdot c^{\frac{a+b-2c}{3}} = (\frac{a}{b})^{\frac{b-a}{3}}\cdot(\frac{b}{c})^{\frac{b-c}{3}}\cdot(\frac{a}{c})^{\frac{c-a}{3}} \\ \because 0\le a\le b\le c \\ \therefore (\frac{a}{b})^{\frac{b-a}{3}}\cdot(\frac{b}{c})^{\frac{b-c}{3}}\cdot(\frac{a}{c})^{\frac{c-a}{3}} \le 1 \\ \]

排序/切比雪夫不等式

4

\[ a,b,c\in R^+,abc=1,求证a^2+b^2+c^2\le a^3+b^3+c^3 \\ 不妨设a\le b\le c \\ 由排序不等式, \\ \left\{ \begin{aligned} a^3+b^3+c^3 = a^2\cdot a+b^2\cdot b+c^2\cdot c \\ a^3+b^3+c^3 \ge a^2\cdot b+b^2\cdot c+c^2\cdot a \\ a^3+b^3+c^3 \ge a^2\cdot c+b^2\cdot a+c^2\cdot b \\ \end{aligned} \right. \\ \therefore a^3+b^3+c^3 \ge \frac{1}{3}(a+b+c)(a^2+b^2+c^2) \ge \frac{1}{3}\cdot 3\sqrt[3]{abc}(a^2+b^2+c^2) = a^2+b^2+c^2 \\ \]
